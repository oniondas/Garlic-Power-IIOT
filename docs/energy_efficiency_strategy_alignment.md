## 3. Enabling High-Impact Technical Interventions

The Strategy Report prioritizes technical interventions based on IRR (Internal Rate of Return). Garlic provides the critical **baseline data** required to justify and monitor these top-tier interventions:

### A. Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)
*   **Strategy Rank**: #1 (IRR: **253%**)
*   **The Problem**: VFDs are most effective on motors running at partial load. Installing them blindly is wasteful.
*   **Garlic's Role**: Garlic monitors **True RMS Current** in real-time. It identifies motors that consistently run below rated capacity (e.g., "IDLE" or low-load states), pinpointing exactly which machines will yield the 253% IRR from a VFD upgrade.

### B. Efficient Pumping Systems
*   **Strategy Rank**: #2 (IRR: **135%**)
*   **The Problem**: Pump inefficiency often stems from mechanical wear, cavitation, or clogging.
*   **Garlic's Role**: Garlic's **Vibration (±16g)** and **Anomaly Score (TinyML)** detect mechanical drift and imbalances early. This ensures pumps operate at peak efficiency and validates the performance of new "Efficient Pumping Systems" post-installation.

### C. Efficient Rewinding of Motors
*   **Strategy Rank**: #3 (IRR: **128%**)
*   **The Problem**: Poor rewinding reduces efficiency. Motors often fail catastrophically, forcing hasty, poor-quality repairs.
*   **Garlic's Role**: By predicting failure via vibration analysis, Garlic allows for **planned maintenance**. Owners can schedule high-quality rewinding (or replacement with IE3/IE4 motors) *before* a breakdown occurs, preserving long-term efficiency.
